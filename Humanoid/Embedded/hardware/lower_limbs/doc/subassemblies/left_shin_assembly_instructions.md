# Left shin assembly instructions

- Preparation: 5 min
- Assembly: 10-15 min

## Requirement:
![](../img/shin_assembly_BOM.jpg)


**3D Printed parts**:
- Shin *(diplayed Leg on the picture)*

**Actuators:**
- 1x Robotis Dynamixel MX28-AT (or MX28-T)

**Cables**:
- 1x 3P 220mm

**Robotis parts:**
- 1x HN07-N101
- 1x HN07-I101
- 12x Nuts M2.5
- 12x Bolts M2.5x6

**Motor configuration:**
- 1x Alimentation 12V
- 1x SMPS2Dynamixel
- 1x USB2Dynamixel or USB2AX
- A computer...


## Assembly instructions:
